Public Notice: Summer Advisory
Date: May 21, 2024
Dear Citizens,
As we embrace the summer season, it’s important to acknowledge the intense heatwaves that have been forecasted across various regions of our country. The Indian Meteorological Department has issued warnings for severe heat conditions, particularly in the western and northern parts of India1. In response to the soaring temperatures and to ensure public safety, several states have taken proactive measures: School Closures: Many states have announced early summer vacations for schools. Please refer to local announcements for specific dates as they may vary by region2. Heatwave Precautions: Citizens are advised to stay hydrated, avoid going out during peak heat hours, and wear appropriate sun protection. Health Advisory: Please be mindful of signs of heatstroke and seek medical attention if you experience symptoms such as high fever, severe headache, or dizziness. We urge everyone to take the necessary precautions during this period. Stay informed about the weather conditions and follow the guidelines issued by health and safety authorities.

For further information, please visit the official IMD website or contact your local government office. Stay safe and take care,

By Indian Education Authority
